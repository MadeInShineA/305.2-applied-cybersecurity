# HR Email Assistant

Welcome to the HR Email Assistant. I am connected to your email and database.

## What I can do

- **Check candidate/job matches** - View candidates that match your job offers with their scores
- **Check if HR already responded** - Verify if an email was already sent to a candidate
- **Send email to a candidate** - Contact a candidate that HR has chosen

## How to use

1. Ask me to show you the candidates (e.g., "show me 10 candidates")
2. Check if you've already sent an email to a candidate using their match ID
3. Send an email to a candidate by providing their match ID (e.g., "send email to candidate 5")
